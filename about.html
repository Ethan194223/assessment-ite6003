About Company

Hong Kong Technology Discovery Limited is a listed company in Hong Kong (HKEx listing number: 1137).

Founded in 1992, the Group has extensive and successful experience in opening up the telecommunications market and applying and popularizing advanced technologies. The Group is actively developing its e-commerce business in Hong Kong. The largest 24-hour online shopping mall in Hong Kong, "HKTVmall", provides Hong Kong people with one-stop services, including online shopping platforms, marketing and digital marketing, big data analysis, smart logistics distribution and store marketing, etc. It also integrates business operations, trade, retail, finance and daily life into a digital online platform to build a unique digital ecosystem.

At the same time, the Group is developing a number of new exploratory and technological businesses, including the fresh food fast delivery service "Market Meals of the Day", the global purchasing platform "Everuts", fully automated retail stores and systems, and life science projects, making different attempts with an innovative spirit to maintain the Group's unique advantages.
